Getting Started

How to Get Involved

Volunteering is simple. Here's how you become a frontline member of the Volunteers4TonyPrest movement.

  1. Register Online or at a Center

    Fill in our volunteer sign-up form with your name, LGA, ward, and contact details. It takes under 3 minutes.

  2. Receive Your Volunteer ID Card

    After registration, you'll be issued an official barcoded Volunteers4TonyPrest ID card valid for your ward and LGA.

  3. Join Your Local Team

    Connect with other volunteers in your ward. Attend a community meeting and receive your first organizing task.

  4. Canvass, Register, Mobilize

    Go door-to-door, register voters, organize community events, and spread the message of real change for Delta.
