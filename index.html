Who We Are

Delta's First Real People Power Movement

Volunteers4TonyPrest is a grassroots movement built to support the governorship campaign of Chief Anthony Tosan Prest. This movement is powered by ordinary citizens across Delta State who want real leadership and real change.

From Warri to Asaba, Sapele to Agbor, thousands of Delta sons and daughters are standing up. We are not a party machine. We are the people.

  • Citizen led volunteer coordination across all 25 LGAs
  • Youth powered organizing in every ward
  • Voter registration and outreach drives
  • Community meetups and town hall events

Organizing Infrastructure

Building Volunteer Centers Across Delta State

Our network of community volunteer centers serves as the organizing backbone of the Prest campaign, giving every Delta citizen a local hub to register, train, and mobilize.

Volunteer Community Centers

Dedicated physical spaces in every LGA where volunteers gather, strategize, and receive training on voter outreach and canvassing.

Barcoded Volunteer ID Cards

Every registered volunteer receives an official barcoded ID card, verifying your identity, your ward, and your role in the movement.

LGA Organizing Hubs

Local Government Organizing Hubs coordinate grassroots activity, ward by ward canvassing schedules, and community engagement events.
