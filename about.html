Our Story

A Movement Born from the People

Volunteers4TonyPrest was founded on a simple belief: that Delta State's future should be determined by its people, not by political godfathers, not by money, and not by machine politics.

This is a citizen led volunteer mobilization effort supporting Chief Anthony Tosan Prest's governorship campaign. We are not affiliated with any party structure. We are community organizers, youth leaders, women's groups, and everyday Delta citizens who believe in a better future.

Our movement operates across all 25 Local Government Areas of Delta State, with ward level teams in every constituency.

Our Mission

Four Pillars of Our Campaign

Every activity we undertake is driven by these four mission pillars. They guide how we organize, who we reach, and what we stand for.

Volunteer Mobilization

Recruiting, training, and deploying thousands of volunteers across Delta State. Every ward should have an organized team before election day. We are building the largest volunteer network in Delta State's political history.

Community Organizing

We don't just campaign we build. Our community organizing efforts create lasting civic infrastructure that empowers Delta citizens long after the election. Town halls, community forums, and LGA level organizing hubs.

Youth Engagement

Young people are the backbone of this movement. We are recruiting young Deltans aged 18–35 to lead ward level teams, run digital outreach campaigns, and serve as frontline ambassadors for the Prest campaign.

Voter Outreach

Voter education, registration drives, and get out the vote operations are central to our strategy. We will ensure that every eligible Delta citizen knows how to register, where to vote, and why their vote matters.

The Candidate

Chief Anthony Tosan Prest

Chief Anthony Tosan Prest is a leader forged in the crucible of community service and civic engagement. With deep roots in Delta State and a track record of building institutions that serve ordinary people, he is the candidate that Delta has been waiting for.

His vision for Delta State is anchored in practical governance: economic development that reaches every LGA, security reform, investment in education and healthcare, and transparent management of Delta's considerable resources.

  • Former community development leader across Niger Delta
  • Founder of multiple youth employment initiatives
  • Advocate for transparent oil revenue management
  • Long standing voice for Delta State autonomy and development
What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Transparency

Community First

Every policy decision should improve life in the local community not just the capital.

Youth Empowerment

Delta's youth deserve opportunities, skills, and a future in their homeland.

Justice

A governor who upholds the rights and dignity of every Delta citizen equally.

Economic Growth

Diversified development beyond oil, agriculture, tech, manufacturing, and trade.

Security

A Delta State where communities are safe, and citizens can live and work in peace.
